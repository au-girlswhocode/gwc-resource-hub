CTF & Competitions

What is a CTF?

Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions are hacking challenges where you solve cybersecurity puzzles to find "flags" — strings hidden in vulnerable systems or files. Categories include: web exploitation, cryptography, reverse engineering, forensics, binary exploitation, and OSINT. They're one of the best ways to build real security skills and look great on your resume!
