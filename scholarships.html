Scholarships & Funding

Financial support opportunities for women in tech — scholarships, grants, and fellowships curated for GWC members.

🏅

Tech & Cybersecurity Scholarships

ISACA Foundation Scholarships ↗

For IT, cybersecurity, and governance students. ISACA is a major professional org in the IT audit and security space — having this scholarship on your resume carries weight.

Women in CS Scholarships ↗

Aggregated list of scholarships specifically for women pursuing computer science degrees and careers.

Adobe Research Women-in-Technology ↗

Scholarship for undergraduate and graduate women working in computer science, electrical engineering, and related fields.

Grace Hopper Conference Scholarships ↗

Funding to attend the world's largest gathering of women technologists. Massive networking opportunity.

🌐

Fellowships & Programs

Palantir Path Scholarship ↗

For women, non-binary, and underrepresented students in STEM. Includes mentorship and a tech conference trip.

Generation Google Scholarship ↗

For women and students with disabilities pursuing degrees in CS or a related technical field.

Code for America Fellowship ↗

Work with governments to improve public services through tech. Great for civic-minded technologists.

🎓

General STEM Funding

Fastweb ↗

One of the largest scholarship search engines. Filter by major, year, and identity to find relevant awards.

Scholarships.com ↗

Free scholarship search with a large database. Useful for finding niche, smaller scholarships with lower competition.

Bold.org ↗

Scholarship marketplace with short-essay awards — some as quick as 250 words. Good for stacking smaller awards.

💡

Application Tips

Start scholarship applications early — many open months before deadlines. Reuse and tailor your personal essays across multiple applications. Emphasize your GWC involvement, any coding projects, and your goals in tech. Some scholarships recur annually, so even if you miss this cycle, bookmark them for next year!
