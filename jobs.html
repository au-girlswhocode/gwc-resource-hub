Jobs & Internships

Job boards, internship listings, and platforms curated to help you land your first (or next) tech role.

🚀

Startup & Tech Job Boards

Wellfound ↗

Startup-focused job board for early-stage opportunities. Great for finding roles at fast-growing companies before they go mainstream.

Welcome To The Jungle ↗

Showcases company culture alongside job postings — helps you understand the team vibe before you apply.

Built In ↗

Highlights jobs at startups and established tech firms, with company overviews and benefit details built in.

LinkedIn Jobs ↗

The most widely used professional network. Set up job alerts for tech roles and connect with recruiters.

Levels.fyi Internships ↗

Transparent salary and compensation data alongside internship listings at top tech companies.

Simplify ↗

Autofills internship and job applications for you. Especially useful for applying to many companies quickly.

🌏

International & Government

Go Overseas ↗

Explore international internships across a range of fields — tech, research, NGO, and more.

NSA Careers ↗

Internships and full-time roles in cybersecurity and intelligence — strong pipeline for AU students in CS.

USAJobs ↗

Official federal government job listings including tech, data, and cybersecurity roles across all agencies.

🎓

Programs for Women in Tech

Google BOLD Internship ↗

Google's internship program specifically for students from historically underrepresented groups in tech.

Microsoft Diversity Programs ↗

Microsoft's internship and early-career programs including LEAP and Explorer — aimed at broadening tech access.

Pathrise ↗

Free mentorship and career coaching to help you land tech offers — they only get paid when you do.

GWC Career Resources ↗

Official Girls Who Code career resources, job board, and community exclusively for GWC alumni and members.

Application Tips

📄

Resume Workshop

Attend our Resume Building Workshop (check Events) or get feedback in Discord.

🔗

LinkedIn Presence

Keep your LinkedIn updated with projects, coursework, and GWC involvement. Recruiters look!

🏆

Build Projects

Personal projects and hackathon wins stand out. Check Certifications to build your skills.

📅

Apply Early

Many big-tech internship apps open in August–September for the following summer. Don't sleep on it!
